DOMAIN ACQUISITION SERVICE

Choose Your Name

So, the domain that you want is occupied by someone else? Or maybe it is being used by a small business? Or maybe there are just ads on it. Either way, you are in perfect hands with Namekart Domain Acquisition Service. Simply let us know the domain you want along with your desired budget range.

We Reach Out To The Ownerst

Once we know what you’re willing to pay for the domain, we will reach out to the current owners to see if he or she is interested in selling. Your identity is kept hidden and they never know who is buying the domain name.

We Negotiate On Your Behalf

If the current owners are willing to sell, we will negotiate on your behalf with them. We use our sectoral expertise along with the knowledge of comparable industry sales to secure the name for the lowest possible price for you.

Dedicated Account Manager

We assign a dedicated account manager to you who is responsible for all your concerns going forward. Transaction is done via Escrow.com, the industry standard. We handle the transferring of ownership rights seamlessly. The domain is readily available to you following completion of the transfer. You just pay 20% of the closing price as our service fee.
